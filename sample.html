About

Computer science is a field where experimentation can be done more than study.But we observed the lack of experimentation in our academic curriculum and initiated this club with an intention of providing an reliable playground for playing with computer science.

Any Idea should come from the neurons of a bright guy and this idea took it's birth in the neurons of Meena while we were returning back from the Mobile Developer Fest organized by Google at Tirupathi and we Nayeem,Gouthami and Chandra mouli joined her in making this possible. This couldn't have been possible without the support of any team member and management of our college. We deeply extend our gratitude to our DEAN DR.Subhramanyam sir,HOD DR.Rudra kumar sir,Nagalakshmi mam,Keerthi mam,Rohiini mam for their immense suppport.

Activities

  • Conducting workshops
    • Bring awareness on various programmer tools
    • Teach cutting edge technologies
    • Teach self learning
  • Competitive programming
    • Make them know the importance of Data structures and Algorithms
    • Promote competitive programming
    • Conducting competitive programming sessions on Hackerrank,Hackerearth,Codechef
  • Team spirit
    • Form programming clubs
    • Teach them team playing
    • Make them aware about various version controlling systems like Git,GitHub

  • Promote experimental learning
  • Inculcate learning by doing way of education
  • Improve programming skills
  • Make programming fun
